Booking Procedure

All reservations are made through the registrar's office, the Guest House office, the website, and the app. Teachers from other universities and state university officers who come to Bhopal on official business and are sponsored by their respective institutions may be accommodated if they write for room reservations. The maximum amount of time you can stay is three days. Before occupying the room, guests must make entries in the register with the guest housekeeper.

** If the University requires the rooms for its own use, such provisional bookings may be cancelled without explanation.

check-In/check-Out process

Check-in anytime and check-out 24 hours from the time of Arrival. However ,for the purpose of accounting a day means 24 house from the time of arrival. Fraction of day will be count as full day.
Ordinary person will be allowed to stay for maximum 5 days only .In exceptional cases the extension may be granted subject to the prior approval of the vice -chancellor depending upon the availability of rooms


In each room, there is an inventory of things for usage in the rooms provided by the guests. Guests are asked to check their belongings before entering the room. The guest is responsible for the proper use and repair of the supplies provided in both the rooms and the guest home. They will be held accountable for any losses or damages incurred during their stay.
